💡 How It Works

  • Parked at airport: Distance calculation is temporarily locked to prevent numbers from jumping due to GPS drift.
  • Weak/No Signal: Airplane cabins block GPS. Place your device close to a window. Data won't update if there's no signal.
  • Blinking Numbers: The app is using estimated math (Synthetic Speed) because the hardware stopped sending speed data.
